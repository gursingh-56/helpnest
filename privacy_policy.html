Privacy Policy

App: X-Ray Positioning Assistant  |  Last updated: April 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how X-Ray Positioning Assistant ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App is designed to function entirely on your device.

  • Camera: The App requests access to your device camera solely to perform real-time body-landmark detection using on-device machine learning (MediaPipe). Camera frames are processed locally on your device and are never saved, recorded, uploaded, or shared.
  • No account required: The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • No analytics or tracking: The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.
  • No internet access: All processing happens locally. The App does not send any data to external servers.

2. How Camera Data Is Used

The camera feed is used exclusively to detect body pose landmarks in real time. This helps the App guide you to the correct patient positioning for radiographic projections. The raw camera frames and any derived landmark data are held in device memory only for the duration of active use and are discarded immediately when you leave the camera screen or close the App.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with third parties. There is no data to share because nothing is collected or stored beyond your device's working memory during active use.

4. Data Retention

Because no data is stored persistently, there is nothing to retain or delete. Uninstalling the App removes all App files from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by radiology students, technologists, and healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us so we can investigate.

Because all processing is local and no data is transmitted, there is no network attack surface for your personal data. We follow standard Android security practices (e.g., requesting only necessary permissions, targeting a recent API level).
