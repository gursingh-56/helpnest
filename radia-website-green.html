What is the correct CR angle for a lateral knee projection?
Rx
For a lateral knee projection, the central ray is directed perpendicular to the IR (0° angle) with the patient in a true lateral position. The knee should be flexed 20–30°, with the IR placed at the level of the knee joint.
Key landmark: Medial femoral condyle should project 5mm posterior to lateral condyle.
📚 Bontrager's Handbook · 9th Ed. · Ch. 6, p.212
